# Welcome to Tremor

**The most comprehensive prediction market data platform.** Tremor gives you instant access to millions of historical market snapshots from Polymarket and Kalshi, updated every 5 minutes, with powerful tools to analyze and understand human forecasts at scale.

## What is Tremor?

Tremor is a complete data platform for prediction markets, designed for traders who need edge, researchers studying collective intelligence, and enterprises building market-based forecasting systems.

### Our Vision

Prediction markets aggregate millions of individual forecasts into actionable probabilities. Tremor makes this data accessible, analyzable, and actionable. Whether you're backtesting trading strategies, studying forecast accuracy, or building applications on top of market data, Tremor provides the infrastructure you need.
